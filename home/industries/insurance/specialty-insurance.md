specialty insurance services?</p><p>Specialty insurance services are niche insurance solutions that provide tailored coverage for complex, high risk, or non standard exposures such as cyber, marine, aviation, professional liability, etc. Insurers within this category need to combine deep domain expertise, customized underwriting, advanced risk assessment, and regulatory knowledge to address unique client needs that fall outside traditional insurance products.</p><p>Risk Coverage What types of risks are covered under specialty insurance?</p><p>Specialty insurance covers complex or high risk exposures that fall within niche insurance segments such as cyber and data breaches, professional liability, marine and aviation, construction, environmental risks, product recall, political risk, terrorism, etc. These require complex risk underwriting and advanced risk assessment.</p><p>Technology Support How does technology support specialty insurance?</p><p>Advanced risk modeling, AI driven underwriting, advanced claims handling, data-driven analytics, and compliance automation for complex risks are some ways in which insurers can use insurance technology for specialty lines.
