SEAMLESS INTEGRATION OF BUSINESS AND TECHNOLOGY WITH PLATFORM ENGINEERING

Mphasis XAAP services integrates software, platform and infrastructure as a service from any environment via a single common platform across the enterprise. This seamless integration of business and technology, powered by advanced Platform Engineering practices, enables organizations to develop, deploy and manage services on a common delivery platform supporting all delivery channels. With our Platform as a service solutions, we enable a single pane of glass for efficient observability and augmenting scale.

Our platform offerings include

  • Platform Design Services: We use design approach to identify business network and ecosystem value streams. Such engagement generates a new business and operating model for an organization’s marketplace offerings. For example, we have delivered new revenue channels under three months for successful global organizations
  • Platform Engineering Services: Engineering is in our DNA, we build platforms to integrate value chain partners to offer new products. We have engineered platforms for our customers in logistics, banking and insurance domains to offer products and services on open and proprietary marketplaces
  • Engineering Innovation Platform [XeiP]: We transform Engineering for our customers by establishing cloud native frameworks, tools and community driven team organization. Our customers have improved ‘quarterly release process’ to ‘on-demand’ hybrid cloud deployments under a year!
  • Developer Experience platform: automating the mundane IT operations activities, enable standardisation and reusability across team through discovery and governance leading to high productivity and code quality.

DELIVERING IMMERSIVE EXPERIENCES WITH PLATFORM AS A SERVICE SOLUTIONS

Cloud first architecture for deployment flexibility with 100% resiliency

Mphasis XaaP runs on standard cloud based architecture, and supports deployment on the platform, either internally or externally. This allows deployment of a part of the platform to edge-of-network points of presence to efficiently support performance requirements.

Cloud first architecture for deployment flexibility with 100% resiliency

Omni channel capability for consistent customer experience

With Mphasis XaaP, organizations can enable customers to begin a transaction on one channel and continue it on another. Channels consume services from a single channel marketplace to eliminate channel application silos, and facilitate consistent customer experience regardless of contact point.

Omni channel capability for consistent customer experience

Single dashboard view

Mphasis XaaP provides a single business dashboard to all contact points for an organization’s customers, and visibility of customer behavior across channel contact points for businesses.

We enable connecting right data at the right time in a self-service consumption environment with our Data Fabric and Virtualization solutions, by designing, building and hosting meaningful analytics model.

Single dashboard view

Tooling for rapid development

By using a single platform mode, a common software development kit (SDK) can be used to develop and maintain business services. This removes the disadvantage of different technology platforms and associated tooling to develop services.

Tooling for rapid development

Standardized process orchestration engines

Many processes within organizations require an orchestration engine to manage session state for a longer running process. In banks, for example, these business process include everything from account opening to the mortgage origination. Mphasis XaaP’s orchestration engine takes the common shared services and choreographs them in to these longer running processes. Such sharing of services with different business processes drives efficiency.

Standardized process orchestration engines

Standard integration tiers for external resources

Mphasis XaaP provides a standard interface when accessing a required resource for all services. This allows seamless access to external systems or data without individual channels having to provide integration. This single channel ‘marketplace’ advantage also helps organizations to drive release management strategies to support functional rollouts.

Standard integration tiers for external resources

Total and secure remote access to on-premises environments

With just a site-to-site connection, Mphasis XaaP delivers applications to users without the need for complex VPN management, expensive leased lines or end device management. We also ensure that applications and data do not leave the secure hosting location.

Total and secure remote access to on-premises environments

Intelligent and automated integration to all interfaces

Through an API-driven integration of external products and services, Mphasis XaaP creates an environment of simplified catalogs, domain prescriptions and solution composers for integrated contextual experiences.

Intelligent and automated integration to all interfaces
