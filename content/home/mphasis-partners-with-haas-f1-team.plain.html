AN IMPACTFUL COLLABORATION TO ACCELERATE
THE NEXT ERA OF SPORTS TECHNOLOGY

As the partnership races into its second season, Mphasis and the TGR Haas F1 Team continue to explore new opportunities at the intersection of advanced technology and elite motorsport. Driven by a shared commitment to precision, speed, and relentless improvement, this collaboration blends Mphasis’ digital and engineering expertise with the demands of world-class motorsport. As the Official Digital Partner, Mphasis aims to support the team’s ambition to leverage technology for performance, operations, and sustained competitiveness.

Enabling Smarter, Faster Decision-Making

MIn one of professional sport’s most complex, data-rich environments, Mphasis expertise in data, automation, analytics, AI, and cybersecurity can help TGR Haas F1 Team to establish critical security guardrails, sharpen race-day strategies, and drive on-track performance with millimeter-perfect precision.

The partnership focuses on establishing a robust technological foundation and applying technology thinking that matches the pace, precision, and pressure of top-tier motorsport, where adaptability and insight are critical to success.

Supporting Digital Foundations On and Off the Track

Beyond the track, the continued collaboration reflects a long-term commitment to broaden and strengthen digital capability across a modern motorsport team. By aligning technology strategy with operational priorities, Mphasis supports TGR Haas F1 Team as it continues to build resilient, scalable digital foundations suited to a high-performance, always-on sporting environment.

Connecting Technology, Motorsport, and Fans

As elite motorsport continues to grow its global audience, Mphasis and TGR Haas F1 Team share a common vision of bringing technology closer to the heart of the fan experience. The goal is to explore and experiment with digital innovation that can enhance engagement, storytelling, and connection, giving fans the thrill of living every race.

ABOUT TGR HAAS F1 TEAM

TGR Haas F1 Team competes at the pinnacle of global motorsport and is recognised as one of the sport’s most internationally followed teams. Founded by industrialist Gene Haas, the team is built on a culture of engineering excellence, innovation, and continuous improvement. Led by Team Principal Ayao Komatsu, TGR Haas F1 Team continues to evolve in one of the world’s most demanding sporting environments.
