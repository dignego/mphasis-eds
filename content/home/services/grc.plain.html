GRC Overview What are GRC services?

GRC (Governance, Risk, and Compliance) services help organizations align business objectives with risk management and regulatory requirements.

They integrate governance frameworks, risk identification and mitigation, compliance monitoring, audits, and reporting into a unified approach, enabling organizations to manage uncertainty, meet regulations, and operate with transparency, accountability, and integrity.

Enterprise GRC Why are GRC services important for enterprises?

GRC services are important for enterprises because they reduce risk, ensure regulatory compliance, and improve decision making while protecting reputation and business continuity.

Mphasis supports GRC through integrated governance frameworks, risk assessments, compliance consulting, audits, automation, and continuous monitoring, helping organizations align strategy with regulations and manage evolving risks effectively.

IT GRC How do IT GRC services improve compliance and risk visibility?

IT GRC services improve compliance and risk visibility by centralizing governance, risk, and compliance data into a unified framework.

They automate risk assessments, control mapping, and evidence collection, while providing real time dashboards and reports. This enables organizations to identify risks early, track compliance status continuously, and give leadership clear, actionable insights across IT and cybersecurity domains.

Digital Transformation Can GRC services support digital transformation initiatives?

Yes. GRC services support digital transformation by embedding governance, risk management, and compliance into new digital processes and technologies.

They provide centralized visibility, automate controls, and manage emerging cyber and regulatory risks. This enables enterprises to innovate confidently, adopt cloud and automation securely, and ensure transformation initiatives remain compliant, resilient, and aligned with business objectives.
