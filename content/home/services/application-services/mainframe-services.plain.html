Overview What are mainframe services and why are they still important?

Mainframe services include operating, modernizing, and optimizing high-performance systems that run core enterprise operations.

They remain necessary because they offer unmatched reliability, security, scalability, and 24/7 processing, and modern mainframes now integrate with cloud, APIs, and AI—ensuring continuity while supporting digital transformation.

Modernization How does Mphasis help modernize legacy mainframe applications?

Mphasis modernizes legacy mainframe applications by preserving business logic, extracting knowledge using Gen AI–powered platforms like Mphasis NeoZeta™ to accelerate re-engineering, reduce cost, and ensure transparency.

Mphasis’ Front2Back™ approach and AI-led methodology ensure non-disruptive transformation, reduced technical debt, faster delivery, and future-ready architecture that integrates seamlessly with cloud, microservices, and modern ecosystems.

Cloud Integration Can mainframe systems be integrated with cloud and distributed platforms?

Yes, mainframe systems can integrate with cloud and distributed platforms. They connect through APIs, microservices, and hybrid cloud architectures, enabling data and workloads to move between mainframes and modern environments without disruption.

This lets organizations keep mainframe reliability while adding cloud scalability and flexibility.

Cost Optimization How do Mphasis Mainframe Services help reduce operational costs?

Mphasis Mainframe Services reduce operational costs by modernizing legacy systems, improving resource utilization, and cutting down on maintenance overhead.

Through automation, cloud migration, business rules extraction, and streamlined workflows, they lower technical debt and reduce the expenses tied to aging infrastructure—resulting in more efficient, flexible, and cost-effective operations.
