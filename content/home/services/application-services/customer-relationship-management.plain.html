CRM — Overview What are CRM services and how do they help enterprises?

CRM services help enterprises centralize customer data, streamline sales, marketing, and service processes, and automate routine tasks. They improve customer satisfaction by enabling personalized engagement, faster responses, and better forecasting.

With unified insights, teams collaborate more effectively, driving higher productivity, stronger customer relationships, and long term business growth.

CRM — Platforms Which CRM platforms does Mphasis support?

Mphasis supports major enterprise CRM platforms including Workday, Microsoft Dynamics 365, Salesforce, Oracle Fusion, and SAP Cloud products.

These platforms enable scalable, cloud ready CRM transformations across industries and are backed by Mphasis’ implementation, modernization, and managed services expertise.

CRM — Modernization How does Mphasis help modernize legacy CRM systems?

Mphasis modernizes legacy CRM systems by extracting hidden business logic, preserving institutional knowledge, and accelerating development using proprietary AI‑powered platforms.

Gen AI‑powered platforms such as Mphasis NeoZeta™ and Mphasis NeoCrux™ enable parallel development, unify data, reduce technical debt, and ensure business continuity.

CRM — Engagement & Retention Can CRM services improve customer engagement and retention?

Yes. CRM services improve customer engagement and retention by enabling personalized interactions, unified customer views, faster support, and proactive communication.

They help organizations foster better relationships, reduce churn, and improve satisfaction through seamless, data driven experiences across sales, marketing, and service teams.

CRM — Integration How does CRM integration support enterprise-wide visibility?

CRM integration enables enterprise wide visibility by unifying data across systems, eliminating silos, and ensuring real time information flow.

Mphasis supports this through API first, microservices driven integration, seamless connectivity between CRM, ERP, and other platforms, and standardized interfaces that deliver consistent, organization wide insights.

These integrated architectures enhance decision making, improve customer 360° views, and strengthen operational transparency across the enterprise.

CRM — Managed Services Does Mphasis provide ongoing CRM managed services?

Yes. Mphasis offers ongoing CRM managed services as part of its comprehensive CRM capabilities, including continuous support, maintenance, optimization, and managed-service frameworks to ensure long term operational efficiency.
