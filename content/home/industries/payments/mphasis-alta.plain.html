We believe that the future of global trade and commerce is dependent on a fundamentally new financial services infrastructure. One that is more intelligent, resilient, flexible, immediate, and transparent.

ALTA is an open, cross-functional innovation program focused on helping reimagine how global supply chains make payments and transfer financial value.

UNLOCKING ENDLESS POSSIBILITIES FOR GLOBAL TRADE

Through new approaches to blockchain, tokenization, cognitive technology, cloud and IOT, we focus on removing complex structural barriers in areas such as foreign exchange, settlements, risk, liquidity management and distribution.

  • Enabling instant and transparent payments across currencies and geographies
  • Reducing reliance on outdated FX and settlement systems
  • Broadening access to digital payments and financing across multiple levels of the supply chain
  • Increasing transparency and flexibility in liquidity management
  • Accelerating digital product and platform innovation

CROSS-SECTOR, COLLABORATIVE, OPEN

ALTA is an open and inclusive innovation program that brings together enterprises and their supply chains, banks and financiers, fintech startups, ISVs, technology and infrastructure providers, and academia.

Jointly, we take a hands-on approach to tackle relevant industry problems and develop cutting-edge technology stacks and disruptive business models.
