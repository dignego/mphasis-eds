The healthcare industry is undergoing a fundamental shift powered by artificial intelligence, data intelligence, and rising expectations for personalized, value-based care. Payers and providers are reimagining how care is designed, delivered, and managed—modernizing core systems, simplifying complex interactions, and activating real-time data at scale to unlock the full potential of AI across the enterprise.

Mphasis enables healthcare organizations to lead this transformation. We help payers and providers build intelligent, customized health plans and digital health solutions that improve access, affordability, and outcomes. By modernizing core platforms, enabling interoperable data ecosystems, and embedding AI throughout operations and care workflows, Mphasis delivers connected, on-demand experiences for members, patients, and providers.

Our solutions drive measurable impact—optimized care delivery, reduced administrative burden, smarter decision-making, and differentiated experiences—helping U.S. healthcare organizations accelerate the shift from volume to value with AI at the core.

Payer and Provider Experience

Mphasis provides solutions that helps providers and payers to connect, transform, and deliver innovative and quality integrated care. Through our Front2Back™ approach and Service Transformation, we enable value-based care and enhance the customer experience with a frictionless consumer engagement connecting the patient, provider, and clinicians through multiple channels while harnessing the patient data from various sources.

Administration Platform Services

Mphasis Javelina® delivers one of the most modern, digitally enabled, and scalable payer claims platforms in the market—backed by deep expertise in the self-funded payer ecosystem.

Designed to help payers operate efficiently under increasing cost pressure and regulatory scrutiny, Javelina® combines platform flexibility, proven performance, and rapid adaptability to support today’s evolving healthcare models.

Enterprise Automation of Healthcare Processes

Mphasis combines Enterprise Automation (intelligent Business Process Management, Robotic Process Automation and Optical Character Recognition), Artificial Intelligence, digital, and Next-Gen Data to limit & simplify repetitive tasks, create connected touchpoints across interaction with patients and providers and improve accuracy, RoI and value to end-customers.

Healthcare Data Platform

Mphasis Next-Gen Data, Analytics, and AI/ML solutions combined with Front2Back™ approach help enterprises mine and leverage the healthcare data to enhance customer experience and accelerate decision-making process for improved care delivery. Mphasis DeepInsightsTM enables providers to leverage well proven predictive models to produce better interactive visualizations by harnessing the power of data. It can help deliver an intelligent and modernized healthcare system that drives the future of value-based care with increased efficiency and revenue, reducing costs, and simplifying KPIs reporting.
