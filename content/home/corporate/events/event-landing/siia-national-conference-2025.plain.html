SIIA's National Conference 2025

Mphasis is excited to showcase its cutting-edge healthcare solutions at SIIA's National Conference 2025, October 12-14, JW Marriott Desert Ridge in Phoenix. Focused on AI, Intelligent Automation, and DataOps our solutions empower providers and payers to unlock insights, personalize care, and drive better outcomes.

Leveraging Kore.ai, we deliver AI-powered virtual assistants that enhance patient engagement, streamline scheduling, and integrate seamlessly with EHR systems, bringing efficiency and empathy to every interaction. Our WorkFusion-powered automation accelerates claims processing, revenue cycle management, and compliance workflows, helping healthcare organizations reduce costs and improve operational agility. Complementing these innovations is our robust DataOps framework, which transforms raw healthcare data into real-time intelligence.

Visit us at SIIA 2025, Booth numbers 301 and 303 to explore how Mphasis is shaping the future of healthcare through AI, automation, and data excellence.
